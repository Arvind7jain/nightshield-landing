Problem

In the rapidly evolving landscape of AI and SaaS applications, enterprises are increasingly concerned about the privacy and security of their data, especially regarding PII, PHI, and customer data exposure to third-party AI models and SaaS applications. Enterprises are reluctant to share data unless they can be assured that it won't be inadvertently leaked or used to train external models.

Key Statistics

  • Gartner Research: By 2025, 60% of organizations will use AI platforms heavily, and 85% of those organizations will cite privacy issues as a significant concern.
  • Statista 2023: The global data privacy market is projected to reach $17 billion by 2025.
  • Ponemon Institute (2022): The average cost of a data breach is $4.35 million, with a 10% increase in costs when AI is involved.
  • McKinsey (2022): 71% of organizations consider data privacy a top priority, but only 23% have a clear data privacy strategy.

Solution

Introducing Nightshield.ai, a robust, scalable, and easy-to-integrate data tokenization and de-identification service. Nightshield.ai offers automated tools that can address the core privacy concerns by redacting, replacing, and anonymizing sensitive data before it is fed into AI models or SaaS applications.

It offers solutions such as Private LLMs, nativising Vector DB and LLMs to your cloud, DLP tools etc.

  1. Advanced Tokenization: Nightshield uses cutting-edge tokenization techniques to replace sensitive information with non-sensitive equivalents without losing data usability.
  2. Powerful Deidentification: Advanced algorithms ensure that data is de-identified and cannot be linked back to individuals.
  3. Private LLMs for enterprises
  4. Seamless Integration: Our APIs are designed to integrate effortlessly with existing AI and SaaS platforms, reducing implementation time and cost.
  5. Compliance: Ensures compliance with stringent data protection regulations such as GDPR, HIPAA, and CCPA.
  6. AI-Proof: Guarantees that tokenized or de-identified data is not used to retrain models, ensuring enterprise control over their proprietary data.
  7. Flexible hosting options: On-prem, on cloud, and SaaS options available.
